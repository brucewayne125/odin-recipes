Kitfo



Ingredients

  • Minced Beef
  • Mitmita
  • Niter Kibe(Ethiopian Butter)
  • Salt
  • Korerima (Ethiopian Cardmom)
  • paper

Steps

  1. Filter out the blood from the meat. You can put the meet in a net and press a rock covered with a plastic bag on it
  2. let it rest for 1hr
  3. In a big pot mix the Mitmita, Korerima, Salt, paper and Niter Kibe
  4. put the stove to low heat and heat up the mixture for two minutes
  5. Add the minced beef and quickly mix it together
  6. depending on how cooked you want it adjust the heat
